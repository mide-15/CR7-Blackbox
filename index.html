Career

Ronaldo began his professional career with Sporting CP in 2002, before moving to Manchester United in 2003. He spent six seasons at Old Trafford, winning three Premier League titles, one Champions League title, and one FIFA Club World Cup. In 2009, he moved to Real Madrid, where he won two La Liga titles, two Champions League titles, and two FIFA Club World Cups. In 2018, he moved to Juventus, where he won the Serie A title in his first season.

Skills

Ronaldo is known for his incredible ball control, lightning-fast speed, and powerful shot. He is also a deadly free-kick taker and a skilled dribbler. His work rate and fitness levels are legendary, and he is known for his ability to score goals from almost any position on the pitch.

Achievements

Ronaldo has won five Ballon d'Or awards, the most of any player in history. He has also won four European Golden Shoes, given to the top scorer in European football. He has won numerous league titles, Champions League titles, and FIFA Club World Cups. He has also won two UEFA Euro titles with Portugal, including in 2016 when he was named player of the tournament.
